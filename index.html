About us

Welcome to Praqrti, a website dedicated to providing accurate and detailed information about trees. Our mission is to promote environmental awareness and conservation efforts by helping people better understand the importance of trees in our world.

At Praqrti, we believe that knowledge is power, and we want to empower people to make informed decisions when it comes to trees. Our website is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to everyone. With just a simple scan, you can access a wealth of information about the tree you're looking at, including its species, age, height, and more.

Our team of experts is passionate about trees and dedicated to providing the most up-to-date and reliable information possible. We work tirelessly to ensure that our database is accurate and comprehensive, so you can trust the information you find on our website.

Whether you're a nature lover, a student, a researcher, or just someone who wants to learn more about trees, Praqrti is the perfect resource for you. We invite you to explore our website, learn about the different types of trees, and discover the many ways in which they contribute to our world.

Thank you for choosing Praqrti, and we look forward to helping you discover the wonders of trees!
